ABOUT JACKRABBIT

JackRabbit Systems, Inc. is a leader in providing direct online booking solutions for the travel and tourism industry. Working closely with hundreds of city and state tourism destinations (DMO), and strategically partnering with other industry leaders, JackRabbit offers a variety of solutions and innovative products to meet the needs of our clients. JackRabbit's Book › Direct Search Engine product currently helps over one million consumers each month book their stay directly through our clients' websites. JackRabbit's recently launched Meta › Direct solution has become our fastest growing product to date, allowing lodging partners to easily compete with online travel agencies in top metasearch results.
